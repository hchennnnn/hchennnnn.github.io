Future career for student

Wild Chicken University have special graduation standards. Wild Chicken University will give students an assessment related to their majors in their last year of university. Students who pass the assessment will be awarded the title of "Master Of Chicken" and only students awarded this title can graduate from Wild Chicken University. Wild Chicken university will assign jobs to students who graduate based on their majors and most alumni work in fast food restaurants such as KFC,McDonald's and farms. Their average monthly salary can be as high as 2,000 dollar per month.
