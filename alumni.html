Famous alumni

There are many famous alumni in Wild Chicken University, and the most famous one is XuKun Cai. Xukun not only showed extraordinary talent in chicken research, but also played basketball for only two and a half years before he entered the NBA and won the championship. In addition, Xukun is also proficient in singing, dancing, and rap, bringing a lot of joy to his classmates on campus, so his classmates also affectionately call him "Chicken Brother".
